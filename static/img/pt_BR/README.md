# Brazilian Portuguese Images

Place locale-specific images for the Brazilian version here.

## Image Guidelines for pt_BR locale:
- **Style**: Warm, vibrant, social, expressive
- **Tone**: Friendly, enthusiastic, welcoming
- **Colors**: Warm yellows, oranges, greens - energetic and inviting
- **Content**:
  - Large, lively group gatherings
  - People laughing and interacting closely
  - Colorful, festive settings
  - Expressive body language and gestures
  - Casual, relaxed environments (parks, cafes, bars)
  - Personal profile photos showing personality

## Suggested Images:
- `hero.jpg` - Vibrant social gathering, people laughing
- `group-celebration.jpg` - Large diverse group celebrating
- `cafe-meetup.jpg` - Friends chatting at colorful cafe
- `personality-profiles.jpg` - Expressive personal photos
- `community-festival.jpg` - Outdoor social event
